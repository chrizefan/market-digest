---

## Bull Case

1. **5.0% risk-free yield**: BIL yields ~5.0% with near-zero duration risk, near-zero credit risk, and near-zero volatility. In a world where VIX is 23.9 and geopolitical uncertainty is extreme, earning 5% to wait is extraordinary. This is the best risk-adjusted return in the portfolio. *Source: bonds.md, macro.md.*

2. **Regime demands cash**: Geopolitical shock regime playbook says hold cash and commodities, avoid risk assets. Inflationary transition doesn't hurt cash when rates are 5%. BIL benefits from the very conditions causing pain elsewhere. *Source: macro.md regime, investment-profile.md regime playbook.*

3. **Dry powder optionality**: 32%+ in BIL means enormous firepower to deploy when opportunity arises. If VIX spikes above 35 (Hormuz closure) or markets crash on hot CPI, this cash can be deployed at distressed levels. Buffett sitting at $350B cash sends the same signal. *Source: hedge-fund-intel.md, DIGEST.md.*

## Bear Case

1. **Opportunity cost if market rallies**: If Hormuz Protocol resolves and CPI is cool, SPY could rally 5-8% from here. Being 32% cash means missing that. But this is a FOMO argument, not a fundamental one. *Source: us-equities.md technical analysis.*

2. **Real yield erosion**: If headline CPI is 3.5%+, real yield on BIL is only 1.5%. Not zero, but declining purchasing power relative to headline inflation. *Source: macro.md inflation forecast.*

3. **Fed cut risk (distant)**: If Fed signals surprise cuts, BIL yield drops. But this is extremely unlikely given current inflation trajectory. Risk probability: <5% in next 3 months. *Source: bonds.md Fed analysis.*

## Analyst Verdict

**Bias**: Bullish — maximum conviction
**Thesis Status**: ✅ Confirmed — T-002 Higher-for-Longer is the highest-conviction thesis
**Entry**: Always — BIL is the default destination for any position trimmed
**Exit**: Never fully — always maintain minimum 15% cash. Scale down only when VIX < 18 and macro regime shifts to risk-on.

BIL at 30% is the core cash position. Combined with SHY (see below), total cash/near-cash is ~45%. This is appropriate for a geopolitical shock + inflationary regime with VIX at 23.9. Would only reduce if: (a) VIX drops below 18, (b) oil shock resolves, (c) CPI cools to sub-3%.
