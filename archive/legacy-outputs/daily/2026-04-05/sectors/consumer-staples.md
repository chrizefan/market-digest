## Sector Bias

**Overall Bias**: 🟢 Bullish

**Rationale**:
> Consumer staples are the preferred defensive sector right now. XLP is one of the few sectors in a genuine uptrend (above both 50-DMA and 200-DMA). The sector is absorbing defensive capital that would historically go to healthcare (where policy risk is deterring inflows).

## Macro Regime Fit

- **Growth regime**: Staples are acyclical — perform relatively well in slowdowns
- **Rate environment**: Higher rates are a modest headwind (dividend stocks less attractive vs bonds) but staples outperform if rates cause recession fears
- **Inflation**: POSITIVE — staples have the strongest pricing power of any sector. They pass costs through.
- **Risk appetite**: Classic risk-off beneficiary. Currently fulfilling that role.
