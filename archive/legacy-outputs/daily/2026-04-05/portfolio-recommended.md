## Construction Rationale

This portfolio is built for a **Geopolitical Shock → Inflationary** regime where:

1. **Cash is earning 5% risk-free** — there is no urgency to take risk
2. **Energy is the dominant macro trade** — WTI $112 with war premium. XLE + DBO capture this.
3. **Gold provides geopolitical + inflation hedge** — dual tailwind, $4,400 after -16% correction
4. **Staples are the only defensive equity worth holding** — XLP has pricing power in stagflation
5. **Everything else (EM, REIT, crypto, long bonds, cyclicals) is structurally challenged** — either by rates, oil costs, or USD strength

The barbell structure (50% cash + 25% commodities + 25% equity) maximizes optionality: if markets crash, cash deploys. If war resolves, commodity positions unwind into cash. If stagflation materializes, gold/energy benefit.
