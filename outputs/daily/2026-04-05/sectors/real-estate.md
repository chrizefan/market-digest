## Sector Bias

**Overall Bias**: 🔴 Bearish

**Rationale**:
> Real estate is the most rate-sensitive equity sector and is getting crushed by higher-for-longer. Fed Funds at 4.75-5.00% with no cuts expected makes REIT valuations untenable relative to risk-free alternatives. CRE (commercial real estate) continues to face office vacancy headwinds post-pandemic, and residential is frozen by mortgage rates near 7%. This is a structural AVOID until rates begin declining.

## Key Drivers Today

1. **Higher-for-longer**: Every day rates stay elevated is another day of cap rate compression pressure
2. **CRE stress**: Office vacancy rates ~20% nationally. Regional bank CRE exposure a systemic concern.
3. **Mortgage rates**: ~6.8-7.0% 30Y fixed — housing transactions frozen, residential REITs struggling
